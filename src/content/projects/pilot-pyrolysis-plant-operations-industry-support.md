I provide research and technical support for short-term projects requested by industry partners who are seeking
specific outputs from pyrolysis products or detailed analyses of those products.

These projects are conducted using a pilot-scale pyrolysis plant and involve comprehensive process management,
from material preparation to final reporting.

### Core responsibilities

- Preparing feedstock for pilot plant processing.
- Conducting sampling using coning and quartering techniques.
- Drafting detailed project timelines for execution and reporting.
- Overseeing full pyrolysis plant operations during project runs.
- Troubleshooting technical issues in real time.
- Documenting operational and analytical findings.
- Delivering weekly progress reports to project stakeholders.
