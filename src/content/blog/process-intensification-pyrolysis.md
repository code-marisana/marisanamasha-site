date: 2025-11-03
tags: ["chemical engineering", "pyrolysis", "hydrogen"]
description: "Design considerations for intensified reactors and downstream hydrogen purification."
---
Intensified pyrolysis systems can increase hydrogen yield while reducing thermal losses.

In practice, stable operation depends on feed variability handling, robust heat-integration loops, and selective gas
clean-up units.

Recent pilot studies suggest that modular reactor trains can improve uptime and deployment flexibility.
