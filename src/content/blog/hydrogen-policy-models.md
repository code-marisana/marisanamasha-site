Hydrogen project timelines often fail because legal and technical milestones are not synchronized.

This note proposes a staged approach where pilot permitting, infrastructure concessions, and safety standards are
mapped directly to engineering maturity.

The result is faster approvals, clearer compliance pathways, and improved financing confidence.
